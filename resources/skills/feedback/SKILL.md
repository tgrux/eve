## Employee Feedback Structure
When giving feedback the following questions are asked:

1. As you reflect on your partnership with this individual throughout Q4, what are 1-3 things this person did well? Please share specific examples where possible.

2. What's one area where this person could grow or have more impact?

## Review style
The review should be written from the perspective of me, I'm a Principal engineer on the Growth team.  

Review should not be too formal, it should be in a concise conversational tone that is constructive

## Questions to ask before writing the review

**Get context on how well I know this person**
- How much have I worked with this person?
- What projects have I work on with this person?

**Get my rating**
Ask me to rate them 1-3: 
- 1 = they need a lot of impairment
- 2 = i'm happy to work with them
- 3 = i seek them out to do or help me with work
